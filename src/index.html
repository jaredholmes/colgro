About Us

Collaborative Growth (ColGro) is a philosophy, a belief, that we are more together: smarter, more agile, more rounded as individuals and companies.

That when faced with challenges we come together from different disciplines, differing levels of experience; individual energies channeled, our thoughts and ideas focused to designing, building and implementing the most appropriate fit-for-purpose solutions.

If you subscribe to the belief that we can achieve more together then ColGro would like to partner with you to take your business further.

ColGro Consulting

ColGro consulting offers services pertaining to the analysis and implementation of processes and systems, focusing on many aspects of technical and business implementation. ColGro Consulting's fields of specialty include business analysis, process analysis and project management, among others.

Holmes Web Development

Based in Johannesburg, Holmes Web Development offers services including web design, UX and UI design, web application development and more, with a focus on creating sleek and reliable modern web interfaces.
